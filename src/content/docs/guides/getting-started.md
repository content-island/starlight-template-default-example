This page is part of the sample documentation included in your project. Its purpose is to help you understand how to manage and update your content with **Content Island**.

## Next Steps  

### 1. Edit your content in Content Island  
Go to your [Content Island dashboard](https://contentisland.net) and open your project.  
From the **Content** tab, you can:  
- Add new pages  
- Create folders  
- Edit existing content  

### 2. Sync your updates with Starlight  
Whenever you want to bring the latest content into your local Starlight project, simply open a terminal in the root of your project and run:  

```bash
npm create contentisland@latest update
```
